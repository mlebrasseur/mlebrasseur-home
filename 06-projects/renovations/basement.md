## Scope

- Existing basement bachelor is treated as a legal rentable second unit in working files.
- Future concept: expand the bachelor by taking the back portion of the attached garage.
- Goal: create a larger open living/dining area for the bachelor.
- Feasibility must account for legal rentable status, permits, fire separation, CO safety, egress, ceiling height, insulation, heating, ventilation, sound separation, and garage parking/storage impact.
- Coordinate with garage water infiltration, garage drain/basin work, possible garage-ceiling asbestos, and foundation crack work before construction planning.
